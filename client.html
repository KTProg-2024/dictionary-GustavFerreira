Client

noun

cli·​ent | \ ˈklī-ənt

Definition of client

  1. : a person who engages the professional advice or services of another //a lawyer's clients
  2. : customer //a restaurant's clients
  3. : a person served by or utilizing the services of a social agency
  4. : a computer in a network that uses the services (such as access to files or shared peripherals) provided by a server
